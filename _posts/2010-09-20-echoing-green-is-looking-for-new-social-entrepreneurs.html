Echoing Green has provided seed funding and amazing support for Frogtek and nearly 500 other start-up organizations. The online application for the 2011 Echoing Green Fellowships will open in October 2010. Aspiring social entrepreneurs can review the application questions and sign up for application updates.
We cannot understate the tremendous support that Echoing Green has provided Frogtek with. Not only they became the first investor in our project, by offering access to their excellent network they also helped us find two more investors! 
So if you are launching a social venture, I would definitely recommend you apply. The few hours it will take may be the best investment you've ever made!
